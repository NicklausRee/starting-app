About Us

Born out of the aftermath of the devastating floods that swept across Kenya during the intense rainfall period of 2023, ReliefKenya was created by a tech-savvy Kenyan humanitarian. Witnessing the widespread destruction and displacement across multiple counties, I saw the need for a faster, more direct way to channel aid to affected communities. I believe in the power of collective action and the speed of digital solutions to make a real difference when every minute counts.

Our Mission:

To harness the power of crowdfunding to provide swift, efficient, and transparent disaster relief to Kenyan communities in need, with a focus on flood-related crises.
